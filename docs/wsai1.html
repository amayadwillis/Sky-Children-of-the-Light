    Witch's Broom Thankful Event

This challenge is a race. Every day for six days a picture of a random spirit will be posted. The first person to submit a screenshot of the proper spirit will win a heart. After winning once, you cannot win again the same week. Photos are posted at different times every day so that our many different timezones can participate.
